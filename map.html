Interactive Map

The below map provides information on shooting incidents in New York City's five boroughs. The interactive map allows you to see the locations of the incidents and basic information about both perpetrators and victims.
Individual event markers have popups detailing incident id, location, and perpetrators' and victims' details
The clustering of different markers in the same vicinity is color-coded and the color changes with zoom
Based on the high level view of the map, Staten island has the least number of incidents (approx. 776), while Brooklyn and the Bronx have the highest incidents.
